About Me

Welcome to my academic profile! I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Technology at Fudan University. Currently, I am a Summer Research Intern at the University of Chicago in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Lab, directed by Prof. Pedro Lopes.

My research interests primarily lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Machine Learning (GNNs & LLMs), and Brain-Inspired AI. I am passionate about leveraging computational models to solve complex real-world problems, from optimizing wearable sensor designs to analyzing large-scale neural connectomics.
